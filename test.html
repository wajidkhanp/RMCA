About Us

Welcome to Masjid Name. We are a community mosque serving the local Muslim community.

Our mission is to provide a welcoming and inclusive space for all Muslims to come together and worship, learn, and connect with each other.

Our facilities include a main prayer hall, a separate area for women, a library, and a community kitchen.

  • Jummah prayer
  • Tarawih prayer during Ramadan
